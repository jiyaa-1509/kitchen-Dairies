Indonesian Cooking Techniques

Master these methods for authentic Indonesian flavors

Ulek (Spice Pounding)

Traditional method of grinding spices with cobek (stone mortar) and ulekan (pestle) for authentic flavors.

Slow Simmering (Empal Gentong)

Long, gentle cooking in clay pots to develop deep flavors in soups and stews.

Banana Leaf Wrapping

Cooking foods wrapped in banana leaves for aroma and moisture retention (Pepes, Botok).

Sambal Making

Art of creating various chili pastes - raw, cooked, fried - each with different flavor profiles.
