Chinese Cooking Techniques

Master these methods for authentic Chinese flavors

Wok Hei (Breath of Wok)

High-heat stir-frying that gives food the distinctive smoky flavor and aroma.

Red Cooking

Slow-braising meat in soy sauce, giving it a reddish-brown color and rich flavor.

Steaming

Traditional method for dumplings, buns, and fish to retain moisture and nutrients.

Double Cooking

Boiling then stir-frying, used for dishes like Twice-Cooked Pork.
