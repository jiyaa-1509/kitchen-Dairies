Indian Cooking Techniques

Master these methods for authentic flavors

Tadka (Tempering)

Heating whole spices in hot oil or ghee to release essential oils and flavors.

Wet Masala

Grinding fresh ingredients like ginger, garlic, and onions into a paste for curries.

Dum Cooking

Slow-cooking in a sealed pot to allow flavors to meld and ingredients to tenderize.

Tandoor

Clay oven cooking for breads and marinated meats, creating smoky flavors.
