French Cooking Techniques

Master these methods for authentic French cuisine

Mirepoix

Classic flavor base of diced onions, carrots, and celery sautéed in butter or oil.

Braising

Slow cooking meat in liquid (wine, broth) to develop deep flavors in dishes like Coq au Vin.

Pâte à Choux

Mastering choux pastry for éclairs, profiteroles, and other French pastries.

Sauces Mères

The five "mother sauces" of French cuisine: béchamel, velouté, espagnole, hollandaise, and tomato.
