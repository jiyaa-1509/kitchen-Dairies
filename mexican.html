Mexican Cooking Techniques

Master these methods for authentic Mexican flavors

Molienda (Grinding)

Traditional stone grinding of corn for masa, and using molcajete for salsas and guacamole.

Comal Cooking

Cooking tortillas and toasting spices on a traditional clay or cast iron griddle.

Nixtamalization

Ancient process of treating corn with lime to make masa for tortillas and tamales.

Chili Preparation

Toasting, soaking, and blending dried chilies to create complex sauce bases.
